Academic Writing Skills

Academic Writing Skills

Paraphrasing

Paraphrasing consists of two processes mainly: One is close reading, and the other rewriting. Specifically, the following steps can help you paraphrase a text effectively.

Step 1 Read the text you want to paraphrase until you fully understand its meaning, the relationship between different information, and the author’s purpose or intention.

Step 2 Reorganise the information in a new or different order.

Step 3 Rewrite its points in your own words and expressions.

Step 4 Check your paraphrasing against the original.

Step 5 Include a citation.

释义主要包括两个过程：一是细读，二是改写。具体来说，以下步骤可以帮助您有效地复述文本。

步骤1：阅读想要转述的文本，直到完全理解其含义、不同信息之间的关系以及作者的目的或意图。

步骤2：按照新的或不同的顺序重新组织信息。

步骤3：用自己的文字和表达重写它的要点。

步骤4：对照原文检查你的释义。

步骤5：包括引用。

Summarising

Skills for writing a summary

  1. Read the original text carefully and

  2. Take notes on the following:
    * A) The source of the original text (author’s first/last name, title, date of publication, volume number, place of publication, publisher, URL, etc.)
    * B) The main idea of the original text (use your own words)
    * C) Important supporting points (arrange the points in a logical manner)

  3. The first sentence of the summary should include the in-text citation of the source as well as the main idea of the original passage. There are different requirements for summaries at different length. For a summary containing just one paragraph, use a separate sentence to discuss each supporting point. Give 1−2 explanations for each supporting point, and the explanations should be concise and concluded from the original text. For a multi-paragraph summary, each paragraph should discuss a supporting point. Give each supporting point, as the topic sentence, at the beginning of each paragraph.

  4. To avoid plagiarism, use your own words when summarising.

  5. Transitional words and expressions should be used to show how your summary is organised.

  6. The sources of the original texts should be acknowledged in your summaries.

撰写小结的技巧

  1. 仔细阅读原文

  2. 注意以下内容：
    * A) 原文来源(作者姓名、职称、发表日期、卷数、发表地点、出版者、网址等)
    * B) 原文的主要思想是(用自己的话说)
    * C) 重要支撑点(以逻辑的方式排列点)

  3. 摘要的第一句话应包括原文的正文引文以及原文的主要思想。不同长度的摘要有不同的要求。对于只包含一个段落的摘要，使用一个单独的句子来讨论每个支撑点。对每个支撑点给出1 - 2条解释，解释应简明扼要，从原文中归纳总结。对于多段式总结，每一段都要讨论一个支撑点。在每个段落的开头给出每个支撑点，作为主题句。

  4. 为了避免抄袭，在总结时使用自己的话。

  5. 过渡词和表达应该用来显示你的摘要是如何组织的。

  6. 原文的来源应该在你的摘要中得到确认。
